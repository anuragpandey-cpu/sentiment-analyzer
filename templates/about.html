Our Vision

Bridging the gap between educators and students through modern machine learning.

Driven by Data, Crafted for Connection

SentimentAI transforms raw student feedback into a clear emotional map. Institutions collect extensive amounts of textual context that goes unread or is heavily biased. By deploying a deep Bidirectional LSTM, we instantly classify text safely inside your dashboard.

Our mission is simple: provide educators with clean, immediate, and actionable insights so they can focus entirely on what matters most—student growth.
